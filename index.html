I'm currently employed by Hitachi Vantara as a Technical Consultant. I work on the Software Defined Storage (SDS) team at eBay. We are implementing cloud-native storage systems for eBay's internal cloud.

Projects

compsci-readings: A repo to keep track of miscellaneous computer science related readings that interest me. Will be updated whenever there is something new to add and as long as it is useful.

go-mapreduce: A toy implementation of MapReduce in Golang. Worker cluster is simulated on a single local machine using goroutines that communicate via RPC.

scraper-VALERIE: Python web scraper designed to collect specific keywords and corresponding values from a large database of corporate financial reports (about 200,000). Written as a research assistant for Dr. Valerie Li. Uses Scrapy as a scraping framework and SQLite to manage scraped data.

About

I graduated from the University of Chicago (BS ‘21) with a degree in computer science and a minor in creative writing. I love making things that are fast, robust, and fun to use. I'm particularly fascinated by the guts of software—the parts of code that run behind the scenes and make things tick. I'm also passionate about software for social and environmental good.

Within computer science, I'm primarily interested in distributed systems, networks, computer privacy/security (especially in large distributed systems), and parallel computing. I'm also interested in the mathematical provability of algorithms, such as provable correctness in distributed systems and provable privacy. I hope to do work that makes distributed systems more usable, more secure, and more positively impactful.
