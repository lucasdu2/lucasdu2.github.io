About

I'm a computer science graduate from the University of Chicago (BS '21) currently looking for a software engineering role.

I love making things that are fast, robust, and fun to use. I'm particularly interested in the guts of software—the parts of code that run behind the scenes and make things tick. I'm also interested in software for social and environmental good. I would prefer to work on code that touches backend infrastructure, but I am both open to the full range of software engineering positions and confident in my ability to quickly pick up the skills necessary to deliver value.

My other interests include listening to music, reading, and sometimes writing. Sometimes I wish I could be Ken Liu. My favorite song of all time is Step by Vampire Weekend. My second favorite song of all time is Runaway by Kanye West.
