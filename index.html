Hi, I'm Lucas.

I'm a software engineer at eBay.

I graduated from the University of Chicago (BS ‘21) with a degree in computer science and a minor in creative writing. Currently, I am employed as a Technical Consultant by Hitachi Vantara. I work on the Software Defined Storage team at eBay.

I love making things that are fast, robust, and fun to use. I'm particularly interested in the guts of software—the parts of code that run behind the scenes and make things tick. I'm also interested in software for social and environmental good.

My other interests include listening to music, reading, and, at times, writing. Sometimes I wish I could be Ken Liu. My favorite song of all time is Step by Vampire Weekend. My second favorite song of all time is Runaway by Kanye West.
